Getting Started

  1. Import Your Contacts: Click the Import CSV button in the toolbar or drag a CSV file onto the app window. The CSV should include at least a phone column, plus optional columns like name, product, or other fields.
  2. Review Merge Fields: After importing, the header row of your CSV will appear as clickable merge fields. Click a field to insert {{field}} into your message template.
  3. Compose a Template: Type your message in the editor. Merge fields will be replaced with each contact’s data. Example: Hello {{name}}, your order {{product}} ships on {{ship_date}}.
  4. Preview Messages: The preview pane shows each message personalized for every contact. Double-click a row to send an individual test message.
  5. Send All: Click Send All to deliver your messages. Adjust the delay between sends to avoid rate-limits. You may also switch to “SMS-only” mode if you don’t want iMessage used.
  6. Manage Templates & History: Save templates for later, delete old ones, or view a log of past sends in the History window.

Troubleshooting

  • If CSV import fails, ensure the file is plain text with comma separators and has a header row.
  • If Messages.app does not send, check macOS automation permissions under System Settings → Privacy & Security → Automation.
  • For SMS-only sending, disable iMessage on your Mac and enable Text Message Forwarding from your iPhone.
